Popularity:

Times Square in New York City is one of the most iconic and popular destinations in the world. Known for its dazzling billboards, bustling atmosphere, and vibrant energy, it attracts over 50 million visitors annually. Often called "The Crossroads of the World," Times Square is a hub for entertainment, shopping, and tourism, especially famous for its New Year's Eve ball drop celebration, drawing in millions both in person and via broadcast. Its cultural significance and non-stop excitement make it a must-see for travelers.

Distance from Statue of Liberty:

the Statue of Liberty is located on an island in New York Harbor, the actual travel distance, which involves a combination of ferry and land transportation, is closer to 10 miles (16 kilometers). To visit, you'll typically take a ferry from Battery Park, and from there, it's about a 20-30 minute drive to Times Square.

Best Time to Visit:

The best time to visit Manhattan depends on your preferences, but generally, the spring (April to June) and fall (September to November) are ideal.
